Motion Design Work

Software Used: Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, Photoshop, and Illustrator

Stop Motion

The Stop-Motion Animation was a school project that was taking a series of stills (drawings, photos), and watching them in sequence like a flip book. I used still images of myself to create motion and added special effects to create a horror and suspenseful video.

I focused more on the concept of the book. This is a story where Erzebet Bizecka is a countess who is more in fear of growing old due to her mother going into insanity because she couldn’t produce a male heir to Erzebet father. He left her mother where Erzebet mother lost her beauty. Erzebet didn’t want what happened to her mother to happen to her so she later inflicts horrific acts to where she commit murder and have a secret chamber to commit the heinous act. I searched for videos of dark castles because of Erzebet living in a dark castle. I added sounds and edited out the sounds using Premiere Pro and used Photoshop to cut out the image of Erzebet while adding levels to brighten the color of the character.

Broadcast Station Branding

The Broadcast Station project was a project that I had to use news channels such as ABC, CBS, NBC, PBS, and more. I chose WETA Public Television and Classical Music for Greater Washington because I was an intern there. I wanted to create something special for the company. I chose to do a bumper and use characters like “The Cat in the Hat” hat and “Super Why”. This project was done in Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effects and Premiere Pro.

Public Service Announcement (PSA)

The Public Service Announcement (PSA) was a school project that was about taking action and changing opinions. The idea for the project was to research, conceptualize and write a script for my video. I had put together about events that were impactful in the news focused school shootings because of so much violence that was occurring at schools. I wanted to create a video that raised awareness.
