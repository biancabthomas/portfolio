Graphic Design Work

Software Used: Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign

Plynth Business Suite

Development Marketing Sheet

For this school project, the goal was to visit new development homes to establish ideas for my project. I created a front and back development marketing sheet that consists of a home, amenities, floor plan, site map, and an area map. I added a minimalistic logo to give the marketing sheet a more simplistic feel. The floor plan was created on Easyhome Homestyler which is a free website where you can design a floor plan or build a home. The area map and sitemap were both created on Illustrator. I designed a simple logo and chose blue and brown for the Plynth Homes logo to represent sustainability and healthy living while the Catoctin Hollow Creek logo colors orange and maroon represents strength and motivation.

New Homes Guide Full Page Advertisement

The Full Page Ad is a smaller version of the Development Marketing Sheet showing the floor plan and inside the home as well as the information of the home and area.

Ad Series

For the Ad Series, I had to design a series of advertisement of the following categories: kitchen, office or bathroom tools, carpenter tools or article of clothing. I chose stilettos because of my love for fashion. I also had to research on the history of stilettos. I added textures, brushes and gradients to the background.

Infographic Poster

With this school project, I had to inform my audience about a subject that I felt strongly about so I chose Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) because I have a brother with ASD. I researched on the three levels of ASD: Mild, Moderate, and Severe Autism and focused mainly on Severe Autism (also called Nonverbal Autism) which is level 3.
