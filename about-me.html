About Me

My name is Bianca Thomas, I graduated from George Mason University. My major was Bachelor of Individualized Study and my minor was Web Design. With Individualized Study, I chose courses that were graphic design and web design. I love a great challenge and so I am looking for creative roles such as UX Design, UI Design, Motion Design, Web Design, Graphic Design, or Visual Design. My plan is to become a Full-Stack Developer down the road so taking on a creative role, for example, web design would showcase my creative skills of what I'd learned from college and my internship to grow as a designer and to explore more with my skillset.

Currently, I am reworking on my motion design school project that was my second project called: Print to Motion. It was a project that I had to choose an old school project that I designed in one of my Graphic Design class to change from print to motion using After Effects.
